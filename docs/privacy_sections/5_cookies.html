5. DO WE USE COOKIES AND OTHER TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES?

In Short: We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect and store your information.

We use cookies and similar technologies (such as web beacons and pixels) to gather information when you interact with our Services. These technologies help us maintain the security of our Services, prevent crashes, fix bugs, save your preferences, and assist with basic site functions.

We also allow third parties and service providers to use tracking technologies on our Services for analytics and advertising purposes. These third parties may display advertisements tailored to your interests on our Services or other websites.

To the extent these tracking technologies are considered a "sale" or "sharing" under applicable US state laws, you can opt out by submitting a request as described in section 11. Do United States Residents Have Specific Privacy Rights?.

For detailed information on how to refuse certain cookies, please refer to our Cookie Notice.
