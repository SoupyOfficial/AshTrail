6. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?

In Short: We keep your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Notice unless required otherwise by law.

We retain your personal information only as long as it is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Notice. If no longer required, we will delete or anonymize your information. In cases where deletion is not immediately possible (such as when data is stored in backup archives), we will securely store your information and isolate it from further processing until it can be deleted.
