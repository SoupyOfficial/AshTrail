12. DO OTHER REGIONS HAVE SPECIFIC PRIVACY RIGHTS?

In Short: You may have additional rights based on the country you reside in.

Australia and New Zealand

We process your personal information under Australia’s Privacy Act 1988 and New Zealand’s Privacy Act 2020. This notice satisfies the requirements of these Privacy Acts regarding what information we collect, from which sources, for what purposes, and who else may receive your information.

If you do not wish to provide the necessary information, it may affect our ability to provide you with our services, including:

  • Offering you the products or services you want
  • Responding to your requests
  • Managing your account
  • Confirming your identity and protecting your account

You have the right to request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting us in the section "HOW CAN YOU REVIEW, UPDATE, OR DELETE THE DATA WE COLLECT FROM YOU?".

If you believe we are unlawfully processing your personal information, you may submit a complaint to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner or the Office of New Zealand Privacy Commissioner.

Republic of South Africa

South African residents have the right to request access to or correction of their personal information. To do so, please refer to the section "HOW CAN YOU REVIEW, UPDATE, OR DELETE THE DATA WE COLLECT FROM YOU?".
