Frequently Asked Questions

What is AshTrail?

AshTrail is an application designed to help users track and log their smoking habits, providing insights to help monitor and potentially reduce tobacco consumption.

How do I get started?

Download the app from your device's app store, create an account, and begin logging your entries. The app includes an intuitive tutorial to guide you through the process.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All your information is encrypted and stored securely. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more information on how we handle your data.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings in the app or by contacting our support team at soupsterx@gmail.com.

Does AshTrail work offline?

Yes! AshTrail has offline capabilities, allowing you to log entries without an internet connection. Your data will sync when you reconnect.
